The Loch Ness Monster: Fact or Fiction?

The Loch Ness monster, colloquially known as Nessie, is a rumored large marine monster that many claim inhabits a lake in Scotland. Though there have been numerous reported sightings, alleged physical proof, and photographs, most (if not all) of this evidence has been discredited. So, the question remains... is there really a giant creature swimming in this Scottish lake or is this another spooky tale to tell around a campfire?

History

Rumors surrounding the existence of the Loch Ness monster date back to the late third century and continue to thrive today.
